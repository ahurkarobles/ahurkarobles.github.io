Valencia Changes

San Francisco's Mission District has been rapidly and tumultuously changed by the tech startup boom. This is particularly apparent on Valencia Street, one of the neighborhood's main commercial drags. This interactive illustration portrays a small section of Valencia Street between 18th and 19th Streets.
