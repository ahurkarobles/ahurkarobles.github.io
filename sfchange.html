Problem

In low income areas, local corner stores are often the only convienient place residents can buy fresh produce. In investigating this issue, we found that 35% of this produce spoils in the store before it's sold. This results in lost revenue and enviromental waste.

Hypothesis

Highlighting newly-delivered produce in local markets to interested consumers would result in getting highly perishable stock off the shelves quickly, and would also help customers buy produce when it tastes best and will last the longest in their homes.
